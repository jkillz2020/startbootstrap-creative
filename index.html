My Coding Journey

After graduating from Nashville School of Law and passing the BAR in 2012, I was excited to start practicing law and utilizing my shiny new license. I transitioned to an attorney role with the State of Tennessee within the same department where I had been working as an Administrative Assistant while attending law school. I enjoyed it for a while, but felt that I had peaked quickly without much room for growth or promotion from within the department.

I did some soul searching while continuing to serve as Associate General Counsel for the State and decided to seek some way to join my passion for knowledge and contuious growth with my love for technology. My answer came when, through mutual friends, I met a well established software developer and overall tech ambassador at a Halloween Party in 2015. After discussing software and tech and my desires for a change in careers and work culture, I was ready to make the leap. I enrolled at Nashville Software School in January 2016.

From the moment I met my instructors and classmates I knew I had found my tribe. Nashville has been my home my whole life, but it has certainly exploded onto the tech scene in recent years. I feel that my journey has evolved along with the city. I love digging in and really understanding the nuances of the technology and languages that we use. NSS has provided an environment that encourages experimentation and pushing our understanding. This has been and continues to be an incredible experience and I cannot wait to see what doors are opened and what opportunities present themselves in the future!
